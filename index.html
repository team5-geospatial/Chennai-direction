INTRODUCTION

The analysis reveals significant trends in urban land dynamics, highlighting the importance of geometric assessments in urban planning. North: Due to highways and industry, there will be a rapid growth of around 100 units by 2020. East & South-East: Consistent expansion (IT hubs, coastal corridors). South & South-West: Limited growth (heavy urban core, wetlands). Trend: North-centric sprawl; secondary expansion along the coast and east. Action: Protect southern ecologies and increase transit in the north and east. (Adjusted for Chennai; units presumably as a percentage of area/km²)
